Features & Benefits

Dual Nose Clip Design

Two independent aluminum strips provide double the adjustability for a perfect nose fit, eliminating air leakage.

Glasses Friendly

Anti-fog design prevents glasses from fogging up, ideal for daily wear by glasses users.

Secure Seal

Enhanced nose seal prevents unfiltered air from entering, maximizing protection efficiency.

Easy Adjustment

Dual clips allow separate adjustment of upper and lower nose areas for customized comfort.

Durable Construction

Reinforced nose bridge area maintains shape even after repeated adjustments.

Active Lifestyle Fit

Secure fit stays in place during movement, speaking, and daily activities.
