How to Reach Our Factory

By Air

Recommended Airport: Wuhan Tianhe International Airport (WUH)

  • Fly directly to Wuhan Tianhe Airport (major international hub)
  • From airport to factory: 110km (approximately 1.5 hours by car)
  • We provide airport pickup service upon request
  • Direct flights from major Asian, European, and Middle Eastern cities

By High-Speed Rail

Recommended Station: Wuhan Railway Station or Hankou Railway Station

  • Take high-speed train to Wuhan (connected to all major Chinese cities)
  • From Beijing: ~4-5 hours | From Shanghai: ~4-5 hours | From Guangzhou: ~3-4 hours
  • From Wuhan station to factory: 1.5-2 hours by car
  • Station pickup service available with prior arrangement

Local Transportation

From Wuhan to Xiantao:

  • By Car: 110km via Wuhan Ring Expressway and Huhan Expressway. Travel Time: Approximately 1.5 hours depending on traffic
  • By High-speed Rail: Take high-speed train from Wuhan Railway Station or Hankou Railway Station to Xiantaoxi Railway Station or Tianmennan Railway Station:~40-60 minutes. Take another 40 minutes to the factory by car
  • Our Service: We arrange all ground transportation upon confirmation of your visit

Travel Tip: We recommend arriving in Wuhan the day before your factory visit to ensure a relaxed schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our company and services

Are you a factory, or a trading company?

We are the factory directly. Established in 2003, we are a certified manufacturer with over 20 years of experience in non-woven products manufacturing.

We are authorized by Chinese law to conduct import and export business, which means you work directly with the source, ensuring:

  • Better pricing without middleman markup
  • Direct quality control from production line
  • Faster communication and decision-making
  • Full transparency in manufacturing process

What do you mainly produce?

We specialize in disposable medical mask manufacturing with comprehensive capabilities:

  • OEM/ODM Production: Custom masks with your branding and specifications
  • Our Own Brands: 恒佳保for Chinese market; KAEI for Japanese market
  • Custom Branding: Logo printing on mask outer layer (any color as required)
  • Packaging Customization: Individual, retail, and bulk packaging options
  • Product Range: Adult, teen, and kids sizes; 2-4 layer options

What's your production capacity?

Our manufacturing facility operates at high efficiency with substantial capacity:

  • Daily Output: Up to 1.5 million pieces per day
  • Production Lines: 15 fully automated lines
  • Factory Area: 100,000 square meters with 3 modern buildings
  • Clean Room: 100,000-class ISO-certified environment
  • Scalability: Can increase capacity based on order requirements

What are your competitive advantages?

We differentiate ourselves through three key advantages:

1. Certified Manufacturing Excellence

  • Chinese government-licensed medical device manufacturer
  • Class 2 medical device registration for disposable masks
  • CE and FDA certifications completed for EU and US markets
  • ISO 9001:2015 quality management system certified

2. Unparalleled Geographic Advantage

  • Located in Xiantao - world's largest non-woven protective equipment production base
  • China's only national non-woven production promotion center
  • National emergency medical protective material production and storage base
  • Complete industrial chain integration from raw materials to finished products

3. 20+ Years of Manufacturing Expertise

  • Established in 2003 with continuous operation since
  • One of the largest mask exporters to Japan (known for stringent quality standards)
  • Deep understanding of every detail in the supply chain
  • Proven track record with international clients
